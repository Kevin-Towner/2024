ThanksLink Panel UI - JPN Partner

04.2024

ThanksLink is a travel reservation platform that offers insurance products. With an expressed interest in hosting Travel Guard TI, ThanksLink would like to serve a standard insurance panel for various umbrella partners.

Duration:

Ongoing

Team:

Myself

Functon:

UI Design, Web Design, CM

Overview

This is an openHTML setup, with pre-existing design and front end markup that was already approved by the business. I was tasked with designing a new panel UI to have in our back pocket for demoing the possibilities of optimization/consultation, and showcasing our team's out-of-the-box capabilities.

Alternate UI
