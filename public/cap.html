Competitive Analysis

Conduct a competitive analysis on other insurance panels to build a baseline offer to test upon.


Included in the analysis are screenshots of the various panel content states, such as "Yes" and "No" selections, copy text from the insurance panels, and the completed form data from the booking path.

The example document, shows booking path inputs, copy text, and screenshots. This information is used to not only guide the visual design of the baseline offer, but to also identify the portions of content that could be tested.

Closing Ideas

Considerations

Multivariate testing is a comprehensive method for testing multiple factor combiations against the baseline. It can be quite time consuming to prepare all of the artifacts and QA protocols when publishing large amounts of content for testing. Since content is segmented among a large amount of unique experiences, multivariate testing requires a large amount of traffic, and testing durations can be lengthly.

Ideal Next Steps

Next steps would be to prepare the (16) experiences and configure them per the DOE. In a large organization, this would likely include the use of a CMS and a content testing tool like Adobe Target.
